The Singapura is the smallest breed of domesticated cats.
They are high-energy, pint-sized pets with wide eyes, big ears, and playful personalities.
Learn more about living with Singapuras.

Singapura:

Fun Facts: 

Singapura is the Malaysian word for "Singapore," or "Lion City," where the cat is purported to have originated.
